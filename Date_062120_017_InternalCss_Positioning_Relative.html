position: relative means relative to the original position

left: 250px means left margin would be 250 pixels or x = 250 px. This moves the div 250 pixels to the right

left: -250px means left margin would be -250 pixels or x = -250 px. This would move the div 250 pixels to the left

top: 100px means top margin would be 100 pixels or y = -100 px. This moves the div 100 pixels downwards

top: -100px means top margin would be -100 pixels or y = +100 px. This moves the div 100 pixels upwards






Removing the following lines from CSS:

position: relative;
left: 250px;
top: 100px;

will bring back the div block to its original position, allowing you to see the text.
